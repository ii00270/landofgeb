01  —  The Operating System

One system.
Every task.

The OS is where everything lives. A task enters, gets scoped into hours, gets claimed, gets verified, gets settled. Who's doing what, what's done, what's owed — one screen, one truth. No group chats to dig through, no spreadsheet nobody updates, no boss to chase for your check.

Every hour of confirmed work is a LOG. LOGs are the unit everything else runs on: assignment, reputation, pay.

02  —  Communication

Texting and voice memos are built into the OS. Coordination happens next to the work — not scattered across five apps. Type a message, or hold to record and send your voice. The task hears about it either way.
